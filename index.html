This Privacy Policy explains how TOS — Transport Operating System ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Phone number: Used for login and account creation via OTP.
  • Business data: Job details, client names, vendor names, vehicle information, and billing records you enter into the App.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous usage events (e.g., features used, screens visited) collected via PostHog analytics. No personally identifiable information is sent to PostHog.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's features
  • To send OTP for authentication via SMS
  • To store your transport business records securely
  • To analyse anonymous usage patterns and improve the product

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud database). We use industry-standard security including Row Level Security (RLS) so only you can access your own data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Twilio — SMS OTP delivery (Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered Smart Parser for extracting transport data from messages (Privacy Policy)
  • PostHog — Anonymous usage analytics (Privacy Policy)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above, as required to operate the App.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.
